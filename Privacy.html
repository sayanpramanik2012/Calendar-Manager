Calendar Event Manager - Privacy Policy

At Calendar Event Manager, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

Information Collection and Usage

When you use our application, we may collect certain information from you, including:

  • Your Google account email address for authentication purposes

We use this information solely for the purpose of managing your calendar events and providing the functionality of the application. We do not share or sell your personal information to any third parties.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use industry-standard encryption techniques to secure the transmission of data between the application and the Google Calendar API.

Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the Google Calendar API for event management. Your personal information may be processed and stored by Google as outlined in their Privacy Policy.
